EGYPTAIR CORPORATE PROGRAM

EGYPTAIR corporate program is dedicated to corporations with regular international travel. This
program aspires to help those corporates to expand their business around the world by making travel
easier and ensuring that they are getting the best deal of cost reduction for their annual travel budget.

Your employees will fly on our large fleet using the extensive network around the world seamlessly and
efficiently through an agreement customized to fulfil your travel needs. We are ready to serve you not
only locally through a local corporate agreement but also worldwide through either direct global deals
with EGYPTAIR or Corporate plus agreement -a single agreement covering all Star Alliance member
airlines.

WHAT ARE THE PROGRAM BENEFITS?

  • This program is for your business trips as well as leisure travels.
  • Discounts tailored to your specific corporate travel requirements.
  • Reporting and following your travel needs through an account manager who helps you to
    analyze & manage your corporate travel effectively with continual quality.
  • Reporting and following your travel needs through an account manager who helps you to
    analyze & manage your corporate travel effectively with continual
    quality.
  • Other benefits and advantages related to each corporate situation and travel volume.

WHO IS ENTITLED TO JOIN OUR PROGRAM?

  • Large or medium-sized enterprises may qualify for a corporate agreement.

  • All Entities, Institutions, Banks, Associations, Unions, Chambers and Companies (Medical
    production, Petroleum, Construction, etc.)
